# Digital Personal Data Protection Rules, 2025

## What it is
The subordinate rules operationalizing the DPDP Act, 2023. These rules provide detailed procedures for consent management, data breach notification timelines, cross-border transfer mechanisms, and registration of consent managers.

## Who it applies to
All data fiduciaries and data processors operating under the DPDP Act, 2023.

## Key obligations
- Register Consent Managers with the Data Protection Board
- Implement consent notice format as prescribed in Schedule I
- Report data breaches to the Board within 72 hours of discovery
- Maintain records of processing activities as prescribed
- Conduct Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIA) for Significant Data Fiduciaries
- Appoint independent data auditors for annual compliance audits
- Implement age verification mechanisms for children's data processing
- Follow prescribed procedures for cross-border data transfer
- Respond to data principal rights requests within prescribed timelines
- Maintain processing logs for at least the prescribed retention period

## Maximum penalty
Up to ₹250 crore (as per parent Act)

## Enforcement deadline
Phased rollout expected through 2025-2026
