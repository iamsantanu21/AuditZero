# IRDAI (Insurance Web Aggregators) Regulations, 2017

## What it is
Regulations governing insurance web aggregators — platforms that provide comparison and display of insurance products from multiple insurers, including licensing requirements, operational norms, and consumer protection obligations.

## Who it applies to
Any entity operating or intending to operate as an insurance web aggregator in India.

## Key obligations
- Obtain IRDAI registration before operating as an insurance web aggregator
- Maintain minimum net worth of ₹5 lakh
- Display products from all insurers without bias or preferential treatment
- Not provide advice or recommendation on any insurance product
- Maintain complete records of visitor data and lead information for 3 years
- Implement adequate data security measures to protect customer information
- Comply with IRDAI advertising and marketing guidelines
- Submit periodic reports and returns to IRDAI as prescribed

## Maximum penalty
Cancellation of registration + penalties under Insurance Act, 1938

## Enforcement deadline
In force since 2017
