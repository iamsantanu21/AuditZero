# SEBI Cybersecurity and Cyber Resilience Framework (CSCRF) 2024

## What it is
Comprehensive cybersecurity framework for SEBI regulated entities mandating governance, risk management, security operations, incident response, and audit requirements across all market infrastructure institutions and intermediaries.

## Who it applies to
All SEBI-regulated entities including stock exchanges, depositories, clearing corporations, brokers, mutual funds, portfolio managers, and other market intermediaries.

## Key obligations
- Establish a Cybersecurity Governance framework with Board/senior management oversight
- Implement comprehensive cyber risk assessment methodology
- Set up Security Operations Centre (SOC) for continuous monitoring
- Conduct regular VAPT (at least annually) by CERT-In empanelled auditors
- Report cyber incidents to SEBI and CERT-In within 6 hours
- Implement data classification and data loss prevention controls
- Conduct cyber security awareness training for all employees
- Maintain cyber insurance coverage as prescribed
- Implement business continuity and disaster recovery for cyber incidents
- Submit quarterly cybersecurity compliance reports to SEBI

## Maximum penalty
Up to ₹25 crore per violation + suspension/cancellation of registration

## Enforcement deadline
Implementation from January 1, 2024 (extensions granted for certain categories)
