# RBI Directions on Outsourcing of IT Services

## What it is
Comprehensive directions governing the outsourcing of IT services by banks and NBFCs, covering risk management, vendor due diligence, data security in outsourced arrangements, business continuity, and regulatory reporting requirements.

## Who it applies to
All RBI-regulated entities outsourcing IT services — banks, NBFCs, payment system operators.

## Key obligations
- Board-approved IT outsourcing policy and risk management framework
- Comprehensive due diligence of IT service providers before engagement
- Ensure data stored/processed by service providers remains within India (unless exempted)
- Include mandatory clauses in outsourcing contracts (audit rights, termination, data protection)
- Maintain comprehensive register of all IT outsourcing arrangements
- Ensure business continuity and disaster recovery for outsourced services
- Conduct periodic assessment and audit of service provider security controls
- Report material IT outsourcing arrangements to RBI
- Implement concentration risk management — avoid over-dependence on single provider
- Ensure sub-contracting by service providers is subject to same standards

## Maximum penalty
Up to ₹50 crore + supervisory/regulatory action

## Enforcement deadline
Effective October 1, 2023 (6-month transition from April 2023 notification)
