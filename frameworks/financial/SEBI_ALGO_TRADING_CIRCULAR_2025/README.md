# SEBI Circular on Safer Participation of Retail Investors in Algorithmic Trading, 2025

## What it is
SEBI circular establishing a regulatory framework for retail algorithmic trading, requiring registration of algorithms, exchange-level approval, unique identifier tagging for all algo orders, and safeguards against market manipulation.

## Who it applies to
Stock brokers, algo trading platforms, and retail investors using algorithmic/automated trading strategies.

## Key obligations
- All algorithms must be registered and approved by the exchange before deployment
- Each algo order must carry a unique algorithm identifier
- Stock brokers must obtain exchange approval for each algorithm
- Implement kill-switch functionality for all algo systems
- Conduct simulation and back-testing before live deployment
- Maintain audit trail of all algo orders and modifications
- Implement pre-trade risk controls (order value limits, position limits)
- Third-party algo providers must register with exchanges through brokers
- Regular monitoring and surveillance of algo trading patterns
- Annual audit of algo trading systems and controls

## Maximum penalty
Up to ₹25 crore + suspension of algo trading permissions

## Enforcement deadline
Effective from 2025 (phased implementation)
