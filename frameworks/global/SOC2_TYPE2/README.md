# SOC 2 Type II — Trust Services Criteria

## What it is
AICPA framework for evaluating an organization's controls over security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy. Type II reports cover operating effectiveness of controls over a period (typically 6-12 months).

## Who it applies to
SaaS companies and service organizations, particularly those serving US clients or handling sensitive data. Required by many enterprise procurement processes.

## Key obligations
- Implement controls meeting the five Trust Services Criteria (Security, Availability, Processing Integrity, Confidentiality, Privacy)
- Engage an independent CPA firm to conduct the SOC 2 audit
- Maintain controls operational over the audit period (6-12 months for Type II)
- Implement access controls and logical/physical security measures
- Establish change management and system development processes
- Implement incident response and business continuity procedures
- Monitor system availability and performance against SLAs
- Implement data classification and confidentiality controls
- Conduct risk assessments and vendor management
- Maintain evidence and documentation for all controls

## Maximum penalty
No direct legal penalty — loss of SOC 2 attestation, inability to serve enterprise clients

## Enforcement deadline
No fixed deadline — renewal required annually
