# ISO/IEC 27001:2022 — Information Security Management Systems

## What it is
International standard specifying requirements for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving an information security management system (ISMS). Certification demonstrates commitment to best-practice information security.

## Who it applies to
Any organization seeking to establish an ISMS or achieve ISO 27001 certification — widely required by enterprise clients and in RFPs.

## Key obligations
- Establish an Information Security Management System (ISMS) with defined scope
- Conduct information security risk assessment and treatment
- Define and implement a Statement of Applicability (SoA) for Annex A controls
- Establish information security policy and objectives approved by top management
- Ensure competence and awareness of personnel involved in the ISMS
- Maintain documented information as required by the standard
- Conduct internal audits at planned intervals
- Perform management reviews of the ISMS
- Implement corrective actions for non-conformities
- Continually improve the suitability, adequacy, and effectiveness of the ISMS

## Maximum penalty
No direct legal penalty — loss of certification, contractual implications, reputational damage

## Enforcement deadline
Transition from 2013 edition to 2022 edition by October 31, 2025
