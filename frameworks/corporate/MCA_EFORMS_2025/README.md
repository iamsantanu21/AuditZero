# MCA eForms and Electronic Filing Requirements, 2025

## What it is
Updated electronic forms and filing procedures for corporate compliance on the MCA21 Version 3 portal. Covers annual returns, financial statement filings, event-based filings, and the new simplified proforma returns (SPICe+).

## Who it applies to
All companies registered under the Companies Act, 2013 and LLPs registered under the LLP Act, 2008.

## Key obligations
- File Annual Return (Form MGT-7/MGT-7A) within 60 days of AGM
- File Financial Statements (Form AOC-4) within 30 days of AGM
- File event-based forms within prescribed timelines
- Use digital signature certificates (DSC) for all filings
- Pay prescribed filing fees through the MCA portal
- Maintain compliance with the XBRL taxonomy for financial reporting
- File beneficial ownership declarations (Form BEN-2)
- Comply with MCA V3 portal requirements and digital processes

## Maximum penalty
Additional fees of ₹100/day for delayed filings + penalties under Companies Act

## Enforcement deadline
Ongoing — timelines vary by form type
